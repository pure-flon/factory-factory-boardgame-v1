Checkers (체커)

2-Player · Classic · King Promotion · Mandatory Capture

Red 12
Blue 12
Red's turn — tap a piece to select

How to Play

  • Red moves up, Blue moves down (diagonally on dark squares).
  • Jump over an opponent's piece to capture it. Captures are mandatory if available.
  • After a capture, you may continue jumping (multi-jump).
  • Reach the last row to promote to King (♛) — kings move in all 4 directions.
  • Win by capturing all opponent pieces or leaving them with no moves.
